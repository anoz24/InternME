Why Us
InternMe vs. LinkedIn
1. "Blind Matching" vs. Prestige Bias
LinkedIn's flaw: Search algorithms heavily favor "prestige signals"—elite university names, massive connection counts, and past job titles. It is a popularity contest.
InternMe's edge: Our AI "Blind Match" evaluates pure capability. By stripping away university names and focusing on verified skills, we surface the best talent for the task, democratizing access.
2. End-to-End Transaction
LinkedIn's flaw: LinkedIn stops at the handshake. A company messages a student, and then what? They have to negotiate offline, figure out how to pay them, and risk the student ghosting them.
InternMe's edge: We control the transaction layer. With our Paymob-powered Escrow system, the company deposits the money, the student does the work, and the payout is guaranteed. We aren't a networking site; we are a secure workflow infrastructure.
